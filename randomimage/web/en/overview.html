Surprise Image - Overview

You have lots of great digital photos, but no time so see them? Then you need this app!

This app allows you to select an arbitrary number of images that are stored on your smartphone (or tablet, including SD card) into a list, and then, whenever you want, to get one random photo out of your list displayed.

This does not sound like a great thing, but believe me - it can bring you lots of happy moments! You just have to organize many pleasing pictures into your surprise image list.

For example, you may surprise yourself each day

  • with a photo of your sweetheart
  • with a photo of your last big vacation
  • with a photo of your children when they were young

Just select any photos that make you happy!

Features

The app includes the following features:

  • Add images from the gallery to your surprise image list
  • Display an arbitrary photo from the list on full screen size
  • Zoom the displayed image or switch to another surprise image
  • Place a widget on your home screen that displays a new surprise image in regular time intervals
  • Remove images from the list

If you like the app, you may buy a premium add-on which includes

  • Creation of multiple image lists
  • Backup and restore of image lists
  • Simultaneous use of multiple widgets for different image lists
